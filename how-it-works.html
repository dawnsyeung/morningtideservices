Disciplined deployment

Batch test. Optimize. Operate. Scale.

We stabilize feedstock variability through batch testing and diet management, then build operations around predictable intake and offtake routines.

1) Feedstock review

We document feedstock types, volumes, frequency, handling constraints, and site biosecurity requirements. This defines the pilot design and intake controls.

2) Batch testing & diet optimization

Each stream is batch-tested for conversion, stability, moisture behavior, and handling. We formulate blends and supplementation to stabilize performance before scaling.

3) Pilot operations

Pilots validate throughput, staffing requirements, intake SOPs, and output logistics—so scale-up is operational, not theoretical.

4) Scale deployment

We deploy on-site modular operations or near-site service models, aligned with your site constraints, permitting posture, and offtake goals.

5) Ongoing monitoring

Feedstocks change. We re-test periodically, adjust blends, and maintain SOP discipline to keep conversion and outputs consistent.

6) Offtake value optimization

We manage live larvae staging and logistics, evaluate post-processing pathways, and handle frass as a secondary output aligned to non-pond applications.
