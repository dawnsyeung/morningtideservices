Diet Management & Feedstock Optimization

Aquaculture byproducts are not a stable BSFL diet by default. We batch-test each stream, design blends, and apply targeted supplementation to stabilize conversion, reduce operational risk, and support consistent outputs.

Request Feedstock Evaluation Shrimp Applications

What this service solves

  • Variable protein/fat causing inconsistent larval growth
  • Mineral/ash overload (common in shrimp shells)
  • Salinity variability and moisture swings
  • Texture instability that drives anaerobic breakdown and odor
  • Seasonal drift in feedstock composition

What you get

  • Batch testing plan and acceptance criteria
  • Blend design with inclusion rates by feedstock
  • Supplementation strategy to balance nutrition and digestion
  • Ongoing adjustment protocol as feedstocks change
  • Documentation that supports operations and training

Batch testing

We quantify conversion, stability, moisture behavior, and handling before scaling.

Blend formulation

Ongoing optimization

Periodic re-testing and adjustment to prevent drift and stabilize outputs.
